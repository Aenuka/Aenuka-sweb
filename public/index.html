About Me

A builder with a clear learning path

I started with curiosity, kept going through every stage of study, and turned that interest into a focused path in software engineering.

Software Engineering Undergraduate SLIIT 3rd Year Student

After finishing my O/L exams, I spent my vacation watching HTML tutorials in Chanux Academy and related content from Chanux Bro. That was the point where I realized web development was fun, especially building small web apps and seeing ideas come to life.

After that, I enrolled in a Certificate Course in Computer Science at NIBM and an Advanced English course at the same time, each lasting 6 months. It helped me build both technical confidence and communication skills.

Then I started my A/L studies and took ICT as a subject from 2020 to 2023. After completing my A/L in January 2023, I joined SLIIT for a BSc (Hons) in Information Technology Specializing in Software Engineering. I am now a 3rd year, 2nd semester student and starting my 4th year.
